Presentations

On Friday you will give a presentation to the class. The presentation should be about 10-15 minutes long and should include at least one reference. For this assignment you will give a presentation on how a taxa or coastal system could be impacted by climate change and how you would
1) assess the impact on the organism and/or system without using any molecular biology
2) assess the impact on the organism and/or system with using genomic technigues that could include gene expression and epigenetics mechanisms.
3) include what possible results might look like for both 1 and 2 and how you would interpret them
4) be sure to include any caveats or stipulations (eg “however another explanation might be xxxxxxx)
5) Conclude your talk by discussing the possible implications and what the species or system might look like in 10 year. This could include suggested interventions to change to outcome.
